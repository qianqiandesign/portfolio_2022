Secret Recipe For You

"First Dish I Learned to Cook On My Own"

Ingredients

1 pack Instant Noodle • 12 ounces pork slices
5 scpoons yellow beans • 2 green onions
1 egg • salt & ground paper

Directions

Prep
10 m
Cook
5 m
Ready In
10 m

Boil the water, add the soup base and vegetable mix. Boil for 1 minute.Add the whole disc of dried noodles. Slip the noodles into the boiling broth and press them down with chopsticks or a fork to keep them submerged. At the two minute mark, when the noodles have softened and come apart, pull the noodles from the broth and fan them for two minutes. Add pork shoulder to the mix, but a whole host of other ingredients will give you the depth and nuance you're looking for: chicken necks, spareribs, scallions, carrots, garlic, ginger, and bonito flakes.

INSTANT NOODLE

& PORK SLICE

Ingredients

1 pack Instant Noodle • 3 eggs
5 scpoons soy sause • 2 green onions

Directions

Prep
5 m
Cook
5 m
Ready In
5 m

Prepare the instand noodles as the label directs. Pull the noodles when it is ready and then cut it into pieces. Mix three eggs and add 1 cup of water into the egg and stir them together. Slowly pour the egg misture on the noodle, add soup base and vegetable mix. Put the lid on and warm up it with the microwave for 5 min.

EGG CUSTARD

RAMEN

Ingredients

1 pack Instant Noodle • 2 eggs • 1 baby carrot
1/2 sweet pepper • 2 green onions • salad oil

Directions

Prep
5 m
Cook
3 m
Ready In
5 m

Prepare the instand noodles as the label directs and fan them for two minutes. Cut carrot, sweet pepper and green onion into slices and put them on the noodle. Add two eggs, the soup base and the vegetable mix as much as you want, then stir well. Add some salad oil and bake it for 10 mins.
