Collaborative Editing GTM

Real-time editing was always the most often request from Evernote customers. As the biggest focus of the company in 2022, the Creative Studio led the GTM of the Collaborative Editing project and worked on the campaign that cross video, web, email, blogs, PR and social Media. The theme of this campaign was "where deep work meets teamwork". And my role in this project was the sole designer on web, email, social assets.

INSIGHTS

The release of collaborative editing allows us to expand the Evernote story beyond ‘personal productivity’ to include ‘team collaboration,’ highlighting the ways we can help teams work together seamlessly and simultaneously—all in service of the following goals:

Remove Collaborate Editing as a barrier to booking new Teams accounts

Drive seat expansion for existing Teams accounts

Increase awareness and engagement of collaboration with individual users

Further differentiate the value of Evernote, especially for Pro and Teams plans

While Collaborative Editing helps enable this new story, it’s also table stakes for some of our competitors. For this reason we’ll show the feature’s value within a larger collaboration story.
