Evernote Campaign

The theme for this early summer campaign is “Get a head start to your Happy Place.” Whatever their projects or goals, Evernote provides the tools to get customers where they want to be. We’d like to highlight some of these use cases and connect them to Evernote’s newest features, all while presenting customers with a 40% off Personal annual plan offer. As a result, this 15 days promotion delivered $3MM booking and 10K new subscribers, which was 88% of our last 31 days promotion.

INSIGHTS

In early summer, many customers are looking to relax and reset—often with activities they find personally fulfilling. While the start of the year is about getting your life goals on track, the middle of the year is a time to recharge and achieve the small wins: A calming family get-away, diving into a creative pursuit, finally finishing that home project. Fun as these goals might be, getting there still takes energy and planning. But with Evernote by your side, you can more easily handle the details that’ll get you to your “Happy Place.”

BRIANSTORMING & CONCEPTS

After digging into the brief and the requirement, I had a brainstorming session with the writer and came up with some concepts. Next, we further filtered out the ideas, put the proposed concepts together with moodboard and potential taglines into a slide, and then gave a in-person presentation to gather feedbacks.

The stakeholder loved both two concepts and would like to merge them together. A quick turnaround of mid level concepts was showed with a follow-up meeting. The team decided to move on with Option 2.
