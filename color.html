WEBSITE

Color Theory

Color theory is a website that inspires designers, art students and anyone interested in exploring and creating beautiful visual effects based on the various combination of color theories. Also, it provides a good place for people who desire to learn more about the basic principles of color theory and color implementation in design, branding and fine art.

BACKGROUND

As we all know color theory matters to all design and it tells the audience the expression of the product. However, some websites which teach color theory in the market are poorly designed and improvement needed.


My target audience are designers, art students, and anyone interested in learning about basic principles of color theory or wanted to explore further color implementation in design, branding and fine art.
