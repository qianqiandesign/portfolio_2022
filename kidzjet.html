DASHBOARD & APP

Kidzjet

Kidzjet is a startup company that provides kids with transportation services and kids outdoor science summer camps. To improve the current workflow and offer a better user experience for its customers, Kidzjet decided to design and develop a fully responsive web-based dashboard.

SUMMARY

The primary goal of the dashboard is to provide a complimentary functionality with the Kidzjet mobile app, therefore the schools and parents will have more freedom and flexibility in their daily trip management as well as offering a better user experience.

SITEMAP

Working with the product team, we mapped out all the requirements and defined the scope of the project in a short period of time. After that, I created numerous wireframes and initial mockups that met the business goals and ensured a positive user experience.
